TaRget Audience

The taRget audience for this website is the community in and around the City of Howe. Economic developement and continuity are key goals in making sure the website is accessible and open. Promoting businesses and events that bring business are key. As well as providing an all-in-one resource for permitting questions and information.

Personas

Aspiring Entreprenuer

  • Looking at starting a new business and needs to know what the process is to get started.
  • Wants to know what support the City can offer business starting out.
  • Looks for opportunities to advertise her business with local events.
  • Seeks for available properties and where they are located.

Local Consumer

  • Has a family that he is interested in taking to events and seeks new experiences in the community
  • Uncanny love of Mexican food and wants to see where the nearest place is.
  • Wants to see when events are scheduled so that he can plan ahead to attend.
  • Cares about supporting local small businesses and seeks out members of the community.
