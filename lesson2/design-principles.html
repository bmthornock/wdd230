Clean Design and Whitespace

Codepen

codepen.io

Clean design and plenty of whitespace gives content a visually appealing look by giving content center stage on the page. Plenty of padding and space between content makes the content look organized and uncluttered. The spacing also allows the content to provide contrast drawing the eye to what is important in the page.

Visual Hierarchy

U.S. Beeau of Labor Statistics

bls.gov

Visual hierarchy involves placing emphasis on important items through use of multiple means, like sizing of fonts from most important to least. Visual hierarchy gives a visually appealing appearance as it aims to provide an organized means of giving emphasis on content without ovewhelming or confusing the reader.

Rule of Thirds

Amazon

amazon.com

The rule of thirds is the organization of content in, visual and written, within segments of three equal parts across the page. Rule of thirds gives balance to a page and its content by appealing to natural inclination.
